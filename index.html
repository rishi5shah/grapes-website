About GrapesOne Press

Modern News Distribution for the GCC

Press is a distribution marketplace that helps businesses share announcements, launches, company updates, and stories across publishers, blogs, communities, newsletters, and media channels. Built for startups, agencies, and enterprises, Press makes media distribution more accessible, transparent, and measurable.

Why We Built It

Traditional PR is often expensive, fragmented, and difficult to measure. Press was created to give organizations direct access to distribution opportunities with transparent pricing, clear visibility, and greater control over where their stories are shared.

Built for the GCC

Designed specifically for the Gulf market, Press connects brands with regional publishers, media platforms, communities, and digital distribution channels from a single platform.

Powered by GrapesOne

Press is developed by GrapesOne, the technology division of Grapes Group focused on building marketing, media, and advertising technology solutions.

"To become the leading distribution infrastructure powering business visibility across the GCC."
